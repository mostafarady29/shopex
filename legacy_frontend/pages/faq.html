Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping with SaaS Hub

General Questions

How do I create an account?
Click on the "Login" button in the top right corner, then select "Sign Up". Fill in your details including email, password, and personal information. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.
Is my personal information secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your personal and payment information. We never share your data with third parties without your consent.

Ordering & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and Cash on Delivery for eligible orders.
Can I modify or cancel my order?
You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it. After that, the order enters processing and cannot be changed. Contact our support team for assistance.
Do you offer discounts or coupon codes?
Yes! Subscribe to our newsletter to receive exclusive discount codes and be the first to know about sales and promotions.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 business days) is available for an additional fee. International orders may take 10-15 business days.
Do you offer free shipping?
Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $100. Orders under $100 have a flat shipping fee of $10.
How can I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be unused and in original packaging. Some items like electronics may have different return policies.
How long does it take to process a refund?
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your returned item. The refund will be credited to your original payment method.
